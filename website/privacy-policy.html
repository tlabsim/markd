Privacy Policy

Effective June 21, 2026
Markd is designed as a local desktop application. We do not collect, sell, or transmit your documents, personal information, or usage data.

Information collection

Markd does not require an account and does not collect personal information, analytics, telemetry, advertising identifiers, or crash reports.

The app does not operate a server that receives your files or document contents.

Your files

Markdown files, folders, and local images you open in Markd are processed on your device. Markd accesses them only when needed to display, edit, save, export, or browse content that you select.

Your documents are not uploaded to tlabsim or any third party by Markd.

Local preferences

Markd stores application preferences locally on your device. These may include your selected theme, font, palette, zoom level, recent file paths, editor preferences, and remembered scroll positions.

You can remove this information by resetting or uninstalling the application and deleting its local application data.

External links

Documents can contain links to websites or remotely hosted images. If you choose to open an external link, or if a document references remote content, the destination service may receive standard network information such as your IP address and browser or application request details.

Those services operate under their own privacy policies and are not controlled by Markd.

Microsoft Store

If you install or obtain Markd through the Microsoft Store, Microsoft may process account, purchase, installation, diagnostic, or usage information according to Microsoft's own privacy terms. Markd does not receive your Microsoft account credentials.

Linux packages obtained from another distribution service may be subject to that service's own privacy terms.

Children's privacy

Markd does not knowingly collect information from children or from any other user. Because the application does not collect personal information, no age-specific personal data is retained by us.

Policy changes

If Markd's data practices change, this policy will be updated before those changes take effect. The effective date at the top of this page indicates the latest revision.
